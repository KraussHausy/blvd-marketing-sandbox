How to Use This Template

This is a customizable brand guide template. To make it yours:

  1. Update the :root CSS variables at the top of this file to match your brand colors
  2. Change the Google Fonts link to your brand fonts
  3. Replace all placeholder text ("The Studio", "Your tagline") with your brand copy
  4. Add your logo images to the images/ folder
  5. Fill in the tone-of-voice section with your real brand voice examples

Brand Overview

Write 1-2 sentences describing your brand personality. What's the feeling you want people to associate with your business?

Digital Usage

Guidelines for using the brand across web, social, and third-party tools.

  • ☞ Favicon: Use the icon-only logo at 32x32px
  • ☞ Social profile: Icon on brand background color
  • ☞ Email header: Full logo, max 200px wide
  • ☞ Klaviyo popups: Match brand background color, accent color, and body font
  • ☞ HubSpot forms: Use brand fonts and button colors for consistency
  • ☞ Boulevard widget: Apply custom CSS to match brand palette
  • ☞ Buttons: Dark fill with white text. Hover: accent color
